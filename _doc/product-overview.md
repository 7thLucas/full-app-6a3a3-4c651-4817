# Product Overview

## Product Identity

**Working Title:** AI Story Companion (final name TBD — to be confirmed during onboarding)
**Category:** AI Character & Narrative App
**Differentiated from:** Emochi and comparable AI companion chat apps — this app adds autonomous narrative progression
**Phase:** Initial concept

---

## The Problem

AI character chat apps are purely reactive: the story only moves when the user sends a message. Close the app, and the narrative freezes in place. Users who crave immersive, living stories are left with a world that has no momentum of its own — one that demands constant effort just to stay alive. There is no sense that the characters exist beyond the chat window.

---

## The Solution

An AI-driven narrative engine where the story continues even when the user is offline. Characters take actions, scenes unfold, and new narrative beats are generated automatically on a configurable cadence. The user can step in at any moment — to chat, add context, introduce a new scenario, or redirect what's coming — but is never obligated to. The result is a living story, powered by AI and shaped (not driven) by the user.

---

## Target Users

- Creative fiction enthusiasts who love ongoing, immersive narratives
- AI companion and roleplay app users who want more than reactive chat
- Fans of "living world" games and narrative-driven simulation experiences
- Casual readers and writers who enjoy AI-generated storytelling with optional personal input

---

## Core Features

1. **Autonomous Story Engine** — The AI advances the story at a configurable cadence (default: 3 beats per day), generating scenes, character decisions, and narrative events automatically without user input.
2. **AI Characters** — Pre-built and customizable characters with distinct personalities, backstories, motivations, and behavioral arcs that evolve over time.
3. **Story Intervention** — Users can jump in anytime via chat to add context, introduce new scenarios, redirect the plot, or speak directly to characters. Every intervention shapes what the AI generates next.
4. **Narrative Timeline** — A chronological story log showing AI-generated scenes alongside user interventions — a readable, living record of the unfolding story.
5. **Pacing Controls** — Users set how fast the story progresses: slow burn (1 beat/day), moderate (3/day), or active (6+ beats/day).
6. **Scenario Seeding** — Drop in "what if" prompts or scene starters that influence upcoming story beats without locking in a specific outcome.

---

## Brand & Tone

- **Voice:** Immersive, cinematic, literary — the app reads like a living novel, not a chatbot.
- **Aesthetic:** Premium, editorial, unhurried — calm depth over gamified noise.
- **Emotional promise:** "Your story lives, even when you don't."
- **Not:** Gamified progression systems, purely reactive chat, or impersonal mass-market tone.

---

## Strategic Differentiator

Autonomous narrative progression. Every AI companion in this space waits for the user. This app acts — continuously generating story beats, evolving characters, and building narrative tension — then invites the user to respond, redirect, or simply read along. The story is always moving forward.

---

## Verified Operations (North Star)

**Primary operation:** Story Beat Generated — a scene, chapter, or narrative event produced by the autonomous engine or triggered by a user message.
**Secondary operation:** User Intervention — a user message that actively shapes the ongoing story.

---

- Weekly beats per user: 3 × 7 = **21 story beats / week / user**
- Average engagement per beat: **~8 minutes** of reading and interaction
