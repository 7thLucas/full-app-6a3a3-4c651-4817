Foundation

The gap every character app ignores

The Problem

Nobody's home when you leave.

Character chat apps are purely reactive — they only move when you type. Text-only story apps are a heady blank page: no face, no instant payoff. Either way, close the app and everything freezes.

  • ✕ Character goes silent the moment you go offline
  • ✕ Text-only = high friction, no visual payoff
  • ✕ No memory of who you are between sessions
The Solution

Two doors, one living engine.

Chat Mode: tap a character and you're talking in seconds — visual, in-character, remembered. Story Mode: direct a world that writes itself. In both, the autonomous engine keeps things moving while you're away.

  • ✓ Instant visual 1:1 chat — illustrations at emotional beats
  • ✓ Companion memory recalls your name and milestones
  • ✓ The world keeps moving — and pings you — while you're gone
