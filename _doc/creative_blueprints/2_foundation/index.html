Foundation

The gap every companion app ignores

The Problem

Every story stops
when you do.

AI companion apps are purely reactive — the narrative only moves when you send a message. Close the app, and your world freezes. Characters don't think. Scenes don't unfold. The story has no pulse of its own.

  • ✕ Story freezes the moment you go offline
  • ✕ Characters exist only in response to you
  • ✕ Staying alive demands constant effort
The Solution

A narrative with
its own pulse.

An autonomous AI engine writes your story forward — generating scenes, evolving characters, and building tension on its own clock. Step in any time to shape what happens next. Or simply read along. You're always the author, never the gatekeeper.

  • ✓ Story advances automatically, on a cadence you set
  • ✓ Characters act on their own motivations
  • ✓ Intervene any time — or just read along
