The gap every companion app ignores

The Problem

Every story stops when you do.

AI companion apps are purely reactive — the narrative only moves when you send a message. Close the app, and your world freezes. The story has no pulse of its own.

  • ✕ Story freezes the moment you go offline
  • ✕ Characters exist only in response to you
  • ✕ Staying alive demands constant effort
The Solution

A narrative with its own pulse.

An autonomous AI engine writes your story forward — generating scenes, evolving characters, and building tension on its own clock. Step in to shape what's next. Or simply read along.

  • ✓ Story advances automatically, on a cadence you set
  • ✓ Characters act on their own motivations
  • ✓ You're always the author, never the gatekeeper
