Key Use Cases

One engine, four ways to play

📖

Read-along

User rarely chats — treats the app as an evolving novel that writes itself, checking in to read new beats as they land.

Low intervention
✍️

Co-author

User intervenes often, steering plot and dialogue while the engine fills the gaps and sustains momentum between sessions.

High intervention
🧪

Scenario sandbox

User drops "what if" prompts to test directions, then watches how the story metabolizes them into the next beats.

Experimental
💞

Companion roleplay

User chats directly with a character; the engine keeps that character's life and world moving between sessions.

Relationship-led
