Core Features

Built for living narratives

Text-first MVP
⚡

Autonomous Story Engine

Advances the narrative automatically — 3 beats/day by default — generating scenes, decisions, and events without waiting for you.

🎭

AI Characters

Pre-built and customizable characters with distinct personalities, backstories, and motivations that evolve over time.

💬

Story Intervention

Drop in any time to chat, add context, introduce a scenario, or redirect the plot. Every message shapes what comes next.

📜

Narrative Timeline

A chronological log of every AI-generated scene alongside your interventions — a readable, living record of the story.

🎚️

Pacing Controls

Set story speed — slow burn (1/day), moderate (3/day), or active (6+/day). Your narrative, your rhythm, your life.

🌱

Scenario Seeding

Plant "what if" prompts that influence upcoming beats — without locking in a specific outcome. Shape the future.
