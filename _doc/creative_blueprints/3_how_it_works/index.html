How It Works

A loop that never finishes

Shaped by you — not driven by you.
01

Start a story

Pick or create a character and seed an opening scenario — setting, premise, tone.

02

Engine runs

The autonomous engine generates beats on your cadence — advancing plot and evolving characters, present or not.

03

Step in anytime

Read new beats on the timeline, then optionally chat — add context, redirect, or speak to a character.

04

Story absorbs input

Each intervention feeds the engine, shaping the next beats — without overriding the story's own momentum.

05↺

Repeat, forever

The loop continues indefinitely. The story is never finished — and never frozen.
