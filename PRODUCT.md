# Driftoria — Product Information

## Product Identity
**Name:** Driftoria
**Tagline:** Your story lives, even when you don't.
**Category:** Autonomous AI Story Engine for fiction lovers
**One-liner:** A living story world — not a reactive chatbot. Driftoria continues the narrative on its own; characters and plot evolve independently over time, and the user can drop in anytime to add context, inject a new scenario, or redirect the story.

## The Problem
AI character chat apps are purely reactive: the story only moves when the user sends a message. Close the app and the narrative freezes. Users who crave immersive, living stories get a world with no momentum of its own — one that demands constant effort just to stay alive.

## The Solution
An AI-driven narrative engine where the story continues even when the user is offline. Characters take actions, scenes unfold, and new narrative beats are generated automatically on a configurable cadence. The user can step in at any moment — to chat, add context, introduce a new scenario, or redirect what's coming — but is never obligated to. A living story, powered by AI and shaped (not driven) by the user.

## Target Users
- Creative fiction enthusiasts who love ongoing, immersive narratives
- AI companion / roleplay users who want more than reactive chat
- Fans of "living world" games and narrative-driven simulation experiences
- Casual readers and writers who enjoy AI storytelling with optional personal input

## Core Features (must-nail first)
1. **Autonomous Story Engine** — THE core feature. The AI advances the story at a configurable cadence (default: 3 beats/day), generating scenes, character decisions, and narrative events automatically without user input.
2. **AI Characters** — Pre-built and customizable characters with distinct personalities, backstories, motivations, and behavioral arcs that evolve over time.
3. **Story Intervention** — Users jump in anytime via chat to add context, introduce new scenarios, redirect the plot, or speak directly to characters. Every intervention shapes what the AI generates next.
4. **Narrative Timeline** — A chronological story log showing AI-generated scenes alongside user interventions; a readable, living record of the unfolding story.
5. **Pacing Controls** — Slow burn (1 beat/day), moderate (3/day), active (6+ beats/day).
6. **Scenario Seeding** — "What if" prompts and scene starters that influence upcoming beats without locking an outcome.

## Brand & Tone
- **Voice:** Immersive, cinematic, literary — reads like a living novel, not a chatbot.
- **Aesthetic:** Premium, editorial, unhurried — calm depth over gamified noise.
- **Emotional promise:** "Your story lives, even when you don't."

## Anti-references (what Driftoria is NOT)
- Not gamified progression systems with points/streaks/badges
- Not a purely reactive chatbot
- Not impersonal, mass-market, or loud
- Not Emochi-style reactive roleplay — the differentiator is autonomous progression

## Strategic Principles
- The story is always moving forward — the engine acts, then invites the user to respond.
- Autonomous narrative progression is the single defining differentiator; every screen should reinforce that the world has momentum of its own.
- The user is a shaper, not a driver: presence enriches the story but is never required.
- Primary operation (North Star): **Story Beat Generated**. Secondary: **User Intervention**.
